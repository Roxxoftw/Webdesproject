About this project

This was made because I plan on sending this to my friend who want to get into FF14.

This was also a project for my subject in WEBDES handled by Prof. Abraham Magpantay at FEU Institute of Technology

Design choices

The design that I chose at first was filled with images and backgrounds but I was told
that a simple white background for a simple guide would be better, as it also helps this guide to be a quick alt tab guide when playing.

Targeted Levels

This guide is only useful for level 1-50. Beyond that the game gets harder and this only serves as a general guide
and not a class specific guide. There are tons of guides for specific classes out there. Use those for learning a specific class.
