DPS Guide

Welcome to the DPS guide!

This guide will help you learn all the things that you need to know
but in reality dps classes just need to hit the mobs
as often as they can.

Maximizing Damage

To be honest, dps is a no brainer, except when your playing Ninja, Bard, or Samurai. Those there have
specific rotations that will not be explained here. For class specific guides click this link

ALWAYS APPLY YOUR DoTs!

Dots help you get a boost in dps and effectiveness. It cuts down kill times with healthy sum.

Here you can see that my SAM uses buffs and debuffs
before unleashing big damage.

Ranged DPS

Take note that you are not the same as a melee dps. Stay in range, use AoE skills on 3 or more pulls
Thats it, ranged dps is the easiest job to play in this game
There's an extremely low chance that you will suck.


Rears and Flanks

Dps classes benefit the most out of this. You get bonus damage from some skills when you
manage to hit them on these sides


these sides are your best friend, especially when you are a monk or ninja.

Only melee dps classes benefit from this!



Keeping your self alive

Your healer has tanks prioritized for healing, as much as possible avoid getting hit and use skills like Diversion to keep emnity to a minimum

Hit the flanks and keep out of AoE skills. Don't be too greedy with getting hits in!




AoE skills

Most of the time, the tank will pull a big wave of mobs, and its your job to clear them out fast!
Black Mages, Red Mages, Ninjas, and Summoners can deal with big waves with ease.
Use these skills for 3 or more mob pulls.

Fire II - BLM Doton - NIN Bane - SMN Quick Nock - BRD

These are some of the most effective AoE skills that you will get early on in the game. Spam them like crazy when there are 3+ mobs.

Other Notes

To be honest being a dps does not warrant a guide. Your only job is to avoid getting aggro and deal damage.
